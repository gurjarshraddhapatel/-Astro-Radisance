Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about astrology, our services, and how we can help guide your life's journey.

Vedic Astrology, also known as Jyotish, is an ancient Indian system of astrology that uses the sidereal zodiac and focuses on the Moon's position. Unlike Western Astrology which uses the tropical zodiac, Vedic Astrology considers the precession of equinoxes and provides more accurate predictions. It emphasizes spiritual growth, karma, and the soul's journey through different lifetimes.

Astrological predictions are based on mathematical calculations of planetary positions and their influences. While astrology provides valuable insights and guidance, it's important to understand that it shows tendencies and possibilities rather than absolute certainties. The accuracy depends on the precision of birth data, the astrologer's expertise, and the individual's free will to make choices that can alter outcomes.

For the most accurate birth chart, you need your exact date of birth, time of birth (preferably to the minute), and place of birth (city and country). The time of birth is crucial as it determines the rising sign (Ascendant) and house cusps. Even a difference of a few minutes can significantly change the chart interpretation. If you don't know your exact birth time, we can help you with birth time rectification techniques.

Yes, astrology can provide valuable insights for career decisions. By analyzing the 10th house (career), 6th house (work), and planetary positions, we can identify your natural talents, suitable career paths, and optimal timing for career changes. We also look at planetary transits to determine favorable periods for job changes, promotions, or starting new ventures.

Planetary remedies are specific actions, rituals, or practices designed to strengthen weak or malefic planets in your birth chart. These can include wearing specific gemstones, performing certain mantras, observing fasts, donating to charity, or performing specific rituals. Remedies work by balancing planetary energies and helping you align with positive cosmic influences. They are most effective when performed with faith and consistency.

The frequency of consultations depends on your needs and life circumstances. For major life decisions or during challenging periods, monthly consultations can be helpful. For general guidance and understanding of planetary transits, quarterly consultations (every 3-4 months) are usually sufficient. Annual consultations are recommended for comprehensive life planning and understanding yearly trends.

Astrology can provide insights into relationship patterns, compatibility, and timing for marriage. By analyzing the 7th house (marriage), Venus (love), and Jupiter (marriage significator), we can identify favorable periods for relationships and marriage. However, astrology shows tendencies and opportunities rather than absolute certainties, as relationships also depend on personal choices and efforts.

Free consultations provide basic insights and general guidance, typically lasting 10-15 minutes.
